ABOUT ME


My name is Alyssa Szutz and I am currently a student in the Coding Bootcamp Continuing Education classes for Coding.
I am originally from a small town in Minnesota. I moved to the Charlotte area about 5 years ago. I have enjoyed my time out in the Carolinas by being a stay at home mom for the past five years, and now that my kiddos are growing up and going to school I am pursuing a career path in Coding!
So far in my classes we have done a few projects that I am excited to share on this website. I throughly enjoy the front end and CSS work so far, but am hoping to catch up on the back end as well.
As for my spare time you can usually find me hanging outside enjoying some sunshine, or at many of the wonderful local breweries that Charlotte has to offer. I also enjoy rock climbing, hiking, or hanging out around the pool. As much as I would like to say I miss Minnesota, I am sure glad that North Carolina has more appropriate weather for me to spend more time outside.
